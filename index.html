Notes on Images


These images ar made to make you feel like an ant, and to show people how big, yet detailed nature can be. When people understand how beautiful, big, and detailed nature can be, nature might finally be respected.

My Favorite Images

The photos that you just scrolled through are my favorites from my nature walk. During my nature walk I experimented with a lot of different angles,shadows, and lighting. The photos above are some of my favorite examples. The photo with the flower is of a paracidic plant, That means that this plant does not use photosynthesis, instead it feeds off of the tree that it grows near.
